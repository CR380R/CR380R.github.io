About

SMART Operations is a real-time, hybrid cloud and edge-based computer vision product for improving road safety. The system continuously monitors intersections, collecting historical speed and flow data, and capturing clips of any dangerous behaviour or incidents. It also integrates with signal controllers for real-time decision making and actuation.

In my role as technical lead for this product, I was responsible for designing the overall architecture, handling communication between the device and cloud over 4G, and optimising the detection and processing pipeline to run efficiently on low-powered edge devices.

E-BART: Jointly Predicting and Explaining Truthfulness

Research Project‚Ä¢

ACM Journal Article TTO Conference Paper Github

About

In an age where generating and spreading content is so easy, misinformation is ripe. This research project introduces E-BART, a new model for predicting the truthfulness of a statement, while jointly providing a human-readable explanation for its prediction.

The project involved developing a novel neural network and testing its output on human subjects to determine its efficacy. The results of the research were presented at the 2021 Truth and Trust Online Conference and were published in the ACM Journal of Data and Information Quality.
